Why Calorie Tracking Matters

Every day, the food we consume impacts our body in ways we might not always see immediately. Whether you're trying to lose weight or gain muscle, the number of calories you consume plays a huge role in how your body performs and reacts. Tracking calories helps you understand how much energy you're getting from your food, and how that energy aligns with your physical activity level. If you're consuming more calories than your body needs, they can be stored as fat; if you're consuming too few, your body may struggle to build muscle or have the energy needed to perform well during workouts.
